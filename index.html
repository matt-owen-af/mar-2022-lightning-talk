Just-in-time access for DbUp

What is it?

  • A fundamental security practice
  • Privilege is granted for pre-determined periods of time
  • Privilege is granted on an as-needed basis

Who is impacted?

*Just database and software developers

Why?

  • Reduces risk
  • Deployment changes are more auditable and traceable
  • Compliant with recent company standards

What does it mean for you*?

...an ever so slight change to Octopus Deploy

A wild step appears!

Meanwhile, in your DbUp step...


$username = $OctopusParameters['Octopus.Action[Get SQL Credentials].Output.Username']
$password = $OctopusParameters['Octopus.Action[Get SQL Credentials].Output.Password']

$connectionString = "Data Source=$DatabaseServer;Initial Catalog=YourDatabase;User Id=$username;Password=$password"
& ".\Afc.Your.Database.exe" "$connectionString"
